Copy rows into the experiment-specific observations file (e.g. `research/experiment-001-observations.md`).

## Friction questions (ask after session if appropriate)

- What confused you?
- What felt unnecessary?
- What did you expect that didn't happen?
- What did you understand without explanation?

---

## Confidence questions

- What did you trust without needing to verify?
- When did you stop feeling like you had to reconstruct the project?
- Was there a moment you could simply continue?

---

## Facilitator rules

1. Give URL only — no README, Journey, or orient-project beforehand.
2. Do not coach during the lesson.
3. Do not explain contract vocabulary before the results screen.
4. Record hesitation points and question numbers, not interpretations.
5. Post-test: 3 unseen classification items (optional — record score).
